  • Hyderabad : Reviewed 24 July 2010 Hyderabad is city of historic importance. I have visited famouse Gowalkonda Fort and witnessed excellent Light and Sound Show which was organised by Fort authorities. My favourate in Hyderabad is Hussain Sagar Lake and well managed and maintained Laser show which is example of excellent mark of science and technology. Hyderabadi food specially Biryani both in Veg. and Non Vegetirian are excellent,delitious very nice in taste. Your tour of hyderabad is incomple if you miss biryani of Hyderabad. Hyderabad have excellent religious value which is we witnessed by visiting Srisailum. Nagarjuna Sagar Dam and Srisailum Dam are excellent and nice spot to visit where you will get natural beauty in abundant.
  • Bangalore : Bangalore is a city with Excellent weather, Nice greenery, Tall Trees, and Hill station like Roads, ups & downs. Dislike the rude Police, too many religious places in the busy public places,I like Bangalore very much as the people are friendly lot and corperative. It has a lot of attractions but unfortunately. The food is good and the climate is pleasant. Just love to be here. Beautiful roads, too friendly people, must visit.
    Love you Bangalore.
